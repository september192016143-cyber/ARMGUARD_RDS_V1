Import Rules
  • Rows with duplicate AFSN or telephone number are skipped with a warning — they do not stop the rest of the import.
  • Rows with missing required fields or invalid values are also skipped.
  • Personnel photos and QR codes are generated automatically after each row is saved.
  • The first row is always treated as the header row.
  • Blank rows in the middle of the sheet are ignored.
